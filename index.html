How to get songs from Spotify

Quick — Browser Console

  1. Open your playlist on open.spotify.com
  2. Scroll all the way down to load every song
  3. Press F12 then go to Console tab
  4. Paste this script and hit Enter:
const rows=document.querySelectorAll('[data-testid="tracklist-row"]');const songs=[...rows].map(r=>{const n=r.querySelector('[data-testid="internal-track-link"] div')?.innerText||'';const a=r.querySelector('span a[href*="/artist/"]')?.innerText||'';return{name:n,artist:a}}).filter(s=>s.name);copy(JSON.stringify(songs,null,2));console.log(songs.length+' songs copied!');

Songs are auto-copied to clipboard. Paste in the JSON tab!

Easy — Use Exportify

  1. Go to exportify.net
  2. Login with your Spotify account
  3. Export your playlist as CSV
  4. Upload the CSV file in the CSV tab above
